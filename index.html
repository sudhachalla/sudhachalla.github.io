About

I’m Sudharani Challa, user experience designer and art work enthusiast based in Hyderabad, India.

My 9+ years of experience in UI/UX design has given me an eye for detail, a mind for creativity and a technical aptitude for UI development.

Self-motivation and passion are what feeds my eager to learn front-end technologies. I strive to build responsive websites and intuitive digital experiences. My speciality is working on pixel tricks in Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator and turning them into beautiful, semantic HTML & CSS3 prototype. I have solid experience in Bootstrap, jquery and javascript.

Off the computer, I enjoy playing badminton and paper art work.
